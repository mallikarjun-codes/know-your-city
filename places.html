Famous Places in Belagavi

Discover history, nature, and spiritual landmarks across the city.

HISTORY

Belagavi Fort

A 13th-century fort with temples, mosques, and Jain shrines inside the heart of the city.

NATURE

Gokak Falls

A breathtaking waterfall on the Ghataprabha River, called the Niagara of Karnataka.

HERITAGE

Kamal Basti

A 10th century Jain temple inside Belagavi Fort, famous for lotus-shaped dome carvings.

NATURE

Jamboti Hills

A lush green hill station near Belagavi, origin of the Mandovi River.

SPIRITUAL

Military Mahadeva Temple

A peaceful Lord Shiva temple surrounded by greenery, maintained by the Indian Army.

ADVENTURE

Vajrapoha Falls

A hidden gem waterfall in the forests near Khanapur, best visited during monsoon.

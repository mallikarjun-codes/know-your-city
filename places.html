Famous Places in Belagavi

Discover history, nature, and spiritual landmarks across the city.

Belagavi Fort

HISTORY

Gokak Falls

NATURE

A breathtaking waterfall on the Ghataprabha River, often compared to the Niagara Falls of Karnataka.

Kamal Basti

HERITAGE

A 10th century Jain temple inside Belagavi Fort, known for its lotus-shaped dome carvings.

Jamboti Hills

NATURE

A lush green hill station near Belagavi, famous for its forests, wildlife, and the origin of Mandovi River.

Military Mahadeva Temple

SPIRITUAL

A peaceful Lord Shiva temple surrounded by greenery, maintained by the Indian Army.

Vajrapoha Falls

ADVENTURE

A hidden gem waterfall in the deep forests near Khanapur, best visited during the monsoon.

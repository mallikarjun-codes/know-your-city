About Know Your City

Welcome to Know Your City, your personal guide to exploring Belagavi like never before! 🌆✨

Why Know Your City?

Whether you’re a local resident or a first-time visitor, our platform helps you:

  • 🏰 Discover historical landmarks like Belagavi Fort
  • 🌊 Explore natural wonders such as Gokak Falls
  • 🍮 Taste authentic delicacies like the famous Belagavi Kunda
  • 🎉 Stay updated with local events and festivals

Our Mission 🚀

Our goal is to make exploring your city easy, smart, and fun. With AI-powered answers, you can instantly find the best places, hidden gems, and weekend plans — all in one place.

We are starting with Belagavi, but soon we aim to expand to other cities like Hubli, Dharwad, Bangalore, and beyond.
